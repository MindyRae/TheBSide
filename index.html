The B Side

A refined 25+ R&B lounge where the lights are low, the crowd is grown, and the music feels personal.

Lead Paragraph

The B Side is not just a lounge — it’s a return to intentional nightlife. Created for adults 25 and over, our space blends timeless R&B, soft candlelight, curated cocktails, and an atmosphere designed for connection rather than chaos. If you’ve been searching for a night out that feels elevated yet effortless, this is your room.

The Atmosphere

Imagine velvet lighting in deep amber and plum. A small stage framed in gold where live vocalists reinterpret classics. The DJ moves between 90s slow jams and modern neo-soul without missing a beat. Tables are intimate. Conversations flow easily. The dress code is polished — no athletic wear, no chaos, just clean silhouettes and confidence.

Founder’s Note

I created The B Side because I wanted a space that respected grown energy. After years of nightlife that felt loud but empty, I envisioned something intentional — a place where music sets the tone and people come to unwind, not compete. Every playlist, lighting decision, and layout choice reflects that vision.

“The B Side feels like a secret you only tell your closest friends about. It’s rare to find a space where you can dress well, hear real R&B, and actually enjoy the night without distraction.” — Danielle M.

What We Offer

Weekly DJ nights featuring curated R&B sets. Bi-weekly live performances spotlighting emerging vocalists. Private table reservations for birthdays and intimate celebrations. Signature cocktail menu inspired by iconic R&B eras. A 25+ policy ensures a mature, respectful crowd. Our goal is simple: atmosphere over hype, quality over noise.
