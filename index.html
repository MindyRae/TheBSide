The B Side

A 25+ R&B Lounge Inspired by the Soulful Nights of the 90s

The B Side is a 25-and-over R&B lounge inspired by the soulful club scene of the 1990s. Influenced by the iconic vibe of Natalie’s from the television series New York Undercover, The B Side creates a space where music lovers can enjoy classic R&B in a relaxed and stylish environment. Guests can dance, socialize, and enjoy great music without the chaos of typical nightclub scenes. The goal is simple: bring back the atmosphere of real music, real connection, and unforgettable nights.

The B Side lounge atmosphere is designed for music lovers who appreciate classic R&B, relaxed conversation, and a sophisticated nightlife experience.

About the Founder

The B Side was created from a love of classic R&B and the culture that surrounded it. Many nightlife venues today focus on younger crowds and fast-paced music, leaving few places where people who grew up with R&B can truly enjoy the music they love. The B Side was created to bring that experience back. The goal is to create a welcoming environment where people can relax, connect, and celebrate the music that defined an era.

The Experience

The B Side offers a curated nightlife experience focused on classic R&B and mature social environments. Guests can enjoy DJ sets featuring 90s and early 2000s R&B, themed music nights, and occasional live performances. The lounge atmosphere is designed to be comfortable and intimate, allowing guests to dance, enjoy drinks, and still have meaningful conversations. The venue is intended for guests aged 25 and older who want a sophisticated nightlife environment that celebrates music, culture, and connection.

The B Side also welcomes collaboration with local DJs, artists, and event organizers who share a passion for R&B culture.
