The B Side — For the Grown Crowd Who Still Loves Real R&B

The B Side is a 25+ R&B lounge inspired by the vibe of Natalie’s in New York Undercover — dim lights, real music, good food, and people who came out dressed like they meant it. No college chaos. No Top 40 playlists. Just grown energy and a room that feels like it knows what it’s doing.

The Atmosphere

Imagine velvet lighting in deep amber and plum. A small stage framed in gold where live vocalists reinterpret classics. The DJ moves between 90s slow jams and modern neo-soul without missing a beat. Tables are intimate. Conversations flow easily. The dress code is polished — no athletic wear, no chaos, just clean silhouettes and confidence.

Founder’s Note

I created The B Side because I got tired of going out and feeling like I was too grown for the room. I still love R&B. I still like to dance. I still want to dress up. But I don’t want sticky floors, overcrowded spaces, or music that switches every 20 seconds. The B Side is the kind of place I was looking for but couldn’t find.

“I haven’t heard a room sing along to real R&B like that in years. It felt like stepping into a different era but with better drinks and better food. It wasn’t loud just to be loud. It was intentional.” — Marcus D., Guest

What We Offer

The B Side is a 25 and over R&B lounge experience designed for people who still appreciate music with feeling. Our nights focus on 90s R&B, neo soul, slow jams, and curated sets that make sense from start to finish. Some evenings include live vocalists or local bands. Other nights keep it smooth with a DJ who understands timing and flow. We offer a focused food menu with elevated comfort dishes and cocktails that fit the atmosphere. Dress code is upscale casual. Come clean and put together. No athletic shorts. No slides. Reservations are encouraged for table seating.
